What We Do

There are a lot of child malnutrition nonprofits around the globe working dilligently to fight against child hunger and undernourishment. Keeping track of all the data for thousands of kids that are screened can be a difficult and time-consuming process. THRIVE allows their data to be easily entered, saved and tracked over time, keeping records organized by both country and community name. We take care of the tedious work, so your team can dedicate more time ensuring the next generation is getting the proper nourishment needed to one day lead our world.
